Spectrum Utilisation

Introduction to the research idea on spectrum systems, May 7, 2020

One of the major bottlenecks of the current wireless networking is the spectrum utilization efficiency and this is because spectrum sharing is not incentivized. Research discusses that spectrum should be shared rather than being exclusively allocated. Spectrum sharing is the situation where at least two users or technologies are authorized to use the same portion of the radio spectrum in a non-exclusive manner.

The channel utilization prediction system is a web based application. The main purpose of “Spectrum Prediction System ” is to provide a convenient way for telcos to study channel utilization for optimal utilization to support upcoming spectrum prediction.
